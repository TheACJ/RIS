Formal Basic Education

Our Formal Basic Education program covers the essential subjects such as Mathematics, Science, English, and Social Studies. We follow a modern curriculum that encourages critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills. Our experienced teachers use innovative teaching methods to make learning engaging and effective.

Special Literacy Programs

We believe that literacy is the foundation of all learning. Our Literacy Programs are designed to help students of all ages improve their reading and writing skills. These programs are especially beneficial for adults and young learners who need additional support to achieve literacy proficiency.

Vocational Training

In addition to academic education, we offer Vocational Training programs that equip students with practical skills for various trades and professions. These programs include training in areas such as agriculture, carpentry, tailoring, and information technology. Our vocational training aims to empower students with the skills needed to secure employment and contribute to the local economy.
